Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the easiest way to save money on a dumpster rental?

Get the right size the first time. It's the biggest and most common mistake people make. Talk to us about your project—that five-minute call can save you hundreds.

Do I need to be home for the delivery?

Nope. Just make sure the drop spot is clear and give us precise instructions. We’ll get it right where you want it.

What happens if I go over the weight limit?

You’ll be charged a standard, prorated fee for the extra tonnage. This usually only happens with super heavy materials like concrete, dirt, or shingles. Tell us what you’re tossing, and we’ll help you avoid it.

Can I put a couch and an old stove in the dumpster?

Yes! Furniture and most appliances are good to go. Just make sure anything with Freon (like a fridge) is professionally drained first.

How fast can I get a dumpster?

We’re local, so we’re fast. We can often provide next-day and sometimes even same day dumpster rental service.

How long do I get to keep the dumpster?

Our standard rental period is a flexible 7-14 days, which is perfect for most home projects.
